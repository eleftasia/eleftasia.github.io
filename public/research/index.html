My research

I work on synthetic biology research with Dr. Sudarshan Pinglay at the Seattle Hub for Synthetic biology and on computing education research with Dr. R. Benjamin Shapiro.

Previous research affiliations

Before starting my PhD at UW, I did research in the Leonard Lab at the Center for Synthetic Biology at Northwestern University. I worked with Dr. Hailey Edelstein on characterizing synthetic transcription factors. I was also part of the Xenobot Group Northwestern's Center for Robotics and Biosystems where I worked on simulations of growing rigid body robots.
